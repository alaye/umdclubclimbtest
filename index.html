Who We Are

The Climbing Community at Maryland is a friendly, fearless, driven, enthusiastic, and empowered group of students that have dedicated much of their time and love to the sport of climbing. The Maryland Club Climbing Team was inspired by the climbers themselves and the incredible bond that the community had formed over the years. Whether they were planning trips to the New River Gorge over the summer, working at the rock wall during the school year, or teaching others how to belay, Maryland climbers are constantly looking to discover new places, improve their own skill, and open up these opportunities to other students.
